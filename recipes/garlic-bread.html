Garlic Bread Recipe

Easy roasted garlic bread made with roasted garlic, butter, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

these are the ingredients you'll need to make Garlic Bread:

  • 3 head garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound load italian bread
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 1 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (option)

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until garlic is soft, about 30 minutes.
  5. Set the oven to broil.
  6. Slice bread loaf in half horizontally; place cut-side up on a baking sheet.
  7. Squeeze garlic cloves from their skins into a medium bowl. Stir in butter, Parmesan cheese, and parsley until well blended. Spread onto the cut sides of bread.
  8. Broil bread until toasted, about 5 minutes.
